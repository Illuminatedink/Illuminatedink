About Illuminated Ink

Established in 2019, Illuminated Ink is a custom tattoo studio located in the heart of Dahlonega, Georgia. Owned and operated by professional artist John Mauldin, the shop is built on over 20 years of tattooing experience, a commitment to clean, safe practices, and a passion for bringing bold, meaningful artwork to life.

Whether you’re getting your first tattoo or adding to a growing collection, Illuminated Ink offers a welcoming, professional environment where quality and artistry come first.

Meet the Artist

John Mauldin

With over 20 years of experience in the tattoo industry, John Mauldin is the owner and lead artist at Illuminated Ink in Dahlonega, Georgia. Known for his precision, creativity, and dedication to clean, high-quality work, John has built a strong reputation for delivering tattoos that are both visually striking and deeply meaningful.

John specializes in blackwork, traditional, realism, and fine line styles, blending technical skill with an eye for design to create custom pieces that stand the test of time. Whether it’s bold linework or intricate detail, he approaches every tattoo with care, collaboration, and a deep respect for the craft.

When he’s not tattooing, John continues to refine his skills through drawing, painting, and exploring new artistic styles.
